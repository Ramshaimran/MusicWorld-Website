Symphony of Masters

Ludwig van Beethoven

Classical / Romantic Era

A German composer and pianist, Beethoven was a crucial figure in the transition between the Classical and Romantic eras in music. He remains one of the most recognized and influential musicians of all time.

Notable Works:

  • Symphony No. 5 in C minor
  • Symphony No. 9 "Choral"
  • Moonlight Sonata

Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

Classical Era

An Austrian composer of the Classical period, Mozart showed prodigious ability from his earliest childhood. His influence on Western art music is profound.

Notable Works:

  • The Marriage of Figaro
  • Symphony No. 40
  • Requiem Mass in D minor

Johann Sebastian Bach

Baroque Era

A German composer and musician of the Baroque period, Bach's works are known for their intellectual depth, technical command, and artistic beauty.

Notable Works:

  • The Well-Tempered Clavier
  • Mass in B minor
  • Brandenburg Concertos

Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky

Romantic Era
